## 🌍 ABOUT HAKKA CULTURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The Hakka (客家, Hak-ka = "guest families") are a Han Chinese
group known for:

  • Five great migrations from Central China over many centuries
  • UNESCO World Heritage Tulou earthen buildings in Fujian
  • World leaders: Sun Yat-sen, Lee Kuan Yew, Deng Xiaoping
  • Rich cuisine: salt-baked chicken, stuffed tofu, abacus seeds
  • Strong education values (耕读传家) and perseverance
  • Empowered women who never practiced foot-binding
  • 80 million+ people in a truly global diaspora
